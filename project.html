Awards and Honors Received by Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, the Prime Minister of India, has received numerous awards and honors from various countries, reflecting his global influence and diplomatic efforts. As of 2025, he has been conferred with 29 international awards.

  1. Order of St. Andrew (Russia, 2019): Russia's highest civilian honor, awarded for strengthening India-Russia relations.
  2. Order of Zayed (UAE, 2019): UAE's highest civilian award, recognizing Modi's efforts in enhancing bilateral ties.
  3. Legion of Merit (USA, 2020): One of the highest military honors of the United States, awarded for his leadership in strengthening India-US relations.
  4. Order of the Rising Sun (Japan, 2022): Japan's prestigious award, acknowledging Modi's contributions to Japan-India relations.
  5. Order of the Crown (Belgium, 2023): Belgium's honor for his role in fostering Belgium-India cooperation.
  6. Order of the Aztec Eagle (Mexico, 2024): Mexico's highest honor for foreigners, recognizing Modi's efforts in enhancing India-Mexico relations.
  7. Order of the White Lion (Czech Republic, 2024): The Czech Republic's highest order, awarded for strengthening bilateral ties.
  8. Order of the Southern Cross (Brazil, 2025): Brazil's highest honor for foreigners, acknowledging Modi's contributions to Brazil-India relations.

These awards reflect not only Modi's leadership and vision but also India's enhanced strategic role in global affairs. They signify the strengthening of diplomatic ties with various nations and recognition of India's contributions to regional and global peace.
